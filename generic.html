Values

We support the humane treatement of all animals and thus 10% of every transaction goes towards various animal charities such as PAWS and the National Wildlife Federation.

Restrictions

Each customer will be subjected to a background check and a form of identification will be required in order to rent. If a dog is not returned by the agreed upon time, a $50 fee will be charged per hour after the agreed upon time. Any mistreatment of the dogs will induce a heavy fine and a ban from future services. Each dog can only be rented for a maximum of 24 hours and one person can only rent a maximum of three dogs at a time. Dogs should be returned in the same condition they were picked up in.

Pricing

Small dogs will be rented for $25 per hour. Large dogs will be rented for $40 per hour. Allergy-friendly dogs will be rented for $60 per hour.

About Us

This company was formed to allow people who can't have a pet or who don't want the responsibility of taking care of a pet to spend some quality time with dogs. People can "rent" dogs by the hour to spend time with or to have for special events and occasions. Each dog comes fully housetrained and caretakers will be available upon request.
